1. PSYCHIC NUMBER
What it means:
Your inner personality and how you see yourself. It's calculated from your birth date and shows your natural behavior, thinking style, and daily habits.
How to use your Psychic Number:

Use this number when making daily decisions - if you're confused about small choices, think "does this match my Psychic Number traits?"
Wear clothes or accessories in colors associated with your Psychic Number on important days
Plan your daily routine according to your number's energy (example: if your number is active, schedule important tasks in morning)


2. LIFE PATH
What it means:
Your life's main purpose and direction. Calculated from your full birth date, it shows what you're meant to achieve and the journey you'll take in life.
How to use your Life Path Number:

Use this number when choosing your career field - match your job with what your Life Path Number suggests
Set your life goals based on this number's direction (example: if it says "builder", focus on creating something lasting)
When facing big life decisions, ask "does this align with my Life Path Number's purpose?"


3. EXPRESSION NUMBER
What it means:
Your natural talents and how you show yourself to the world. Calculated from your full name, it reveals your special abilities and professional strengths.
How to use your Expression Number:

Use this number to identify which skills to develop - focus on talents your Expression Number highlights
When applying for jobs, choose roles that match your Expression Number's strengths
In social situations, express yourself in ways your Expression Number suggests (example: if it says "leader", take charge in group settings)


4. SOUL URGE
What it means:
Your heart's deepest desires and what truly motivates you. Calculated from vowels in your name, it shows what your soul really wants and needs.
How to use your Soul Urge Number:

Use this number to make choices that bring inner peace - when unhappy, check if you're ignoring your Soul Urge Number's needs
Create daily habits that fulfill what your Soul Urge Number desires (example: if it craves freedom, schedule alone time daily)
In relationships, communicate what your Soul Urge Number needs to feel satisfied


5. PERSONALITY NUMBER
What it means:
How others see you when they first meet you. Calculated from consonants in your name, it shows the image you project and your outer personality.
How to use your Personality Number:

Use this number to improve first impressions - dress and behave in ways that enhance your Personality Number's image
In interviews or meetings, present yourself according to your Personality Number's strengths
Match your social media presence and personal brand with what your Personality Number projects


6. RELATIONSHIP KEYS
What it means:
Your compatibility with other people's numbers. It shows which numbers work well with yours and which relationships need extra effort.
How to use your Relationship Keys:

Use this number when choosing partners (business or personal) - check if their numbers are compatible with yours
When facing conflicts, understand if it's because of number incompatibility and adjust communication
For hiring or team building, use compatible numbers to create harmony (example: if your number works well with 6, prefer people with number 6)
